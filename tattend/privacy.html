Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

Tattend ("we", "our", or "the app") is a tattoo healing tracker for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

Tattend does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data — tattoos, photos, notes, and settings — is stored locally on your device only.

No Accounts

Tattend does not require user registration or sign-in. There are no accounts, no email collection, and no user profiles.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. We do not track your behavior, location, or usage patterns.

Photos and Camera

Tattend requests access to your camera and photo library solely to let you capture and store tattoo healing progress photos. These photos are stored on your device only and are never uploaded or shared.

Notifications

Tattend may request permission to send local notifications for care and photo reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server.

In-App Purchases

Tattend offers a one-time in-app purchase processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple.

Third-Party Services

Tattend does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Tattend does not knowingly collect data from children. The app does not collect data from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at vitaliywx@gmail.com.
