Common Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to the paywall screen, and tap "Restore Purchase". This will restore your one-time purchase using your Apple ID.

Where is my data stored?

All your data (tattoos, photos, notes) is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud.

How do reminders work?

Tattend uses local notifications for care and photo reminders. Go to Settings inside the app to configure your reminder schedule.

Can I track multiple tattoos?

Yes! You can add and track as many tattoos as you need, each with its own timeline, photos, and notes.
