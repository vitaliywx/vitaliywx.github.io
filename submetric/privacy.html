Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 7, 2026

Submetric ("we", "our", or "the app") is a subscription tracker for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

Submetric does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All your data — subscriptions, categories, notes, and settings — is stored locally on your device.

No Accounts

Submetric does not require user registration or sign-in. There are no accounts, no email collection, and no user profiles.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. We do not track your behavior, location, or usage patterns.

iCloud Sync

If you choose to enable iCloud Sync, your subscription data is synced across your own devices through your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This data is stored in your personal iCloud and is never accessible to us. iCloud Sync is optional and off by default.

Importing from Screenshots

Submetric lets you add subscriptions by importing a screenshot of your list. The text is recognized entirely on your device — no image or recognized text ever leaves your phone or is sent to any server.

Notifications

Submetric may request permission to send local notifications reminding you before a charge or before a free trial ends. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server.

Face ID / App Lock

Submetric can optionally lock the app with Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode. Authentication is handled entirely by the system; we never receive or store your biometric data.

In-App Purchases

Submetric offers a Pro upgrade as an auto-renewable yearly subscription and an optional one-time lifetime purchase, both processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple.

Third-Party Services

Submetric does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Submetric does not knowingly collect data from children. The app does not collect data from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at vitaliy.wx@gmail.com.
